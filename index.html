Mary Jo Hanson: Overview

Mary Jo Hanson is one of Cleveland Ohio's reconized attorneys. I was asked to help recreate her logo, business card, and website to visually display her commitment to giving great advice and consoling to families in need.

Logo Design Process

During my logo design process, I look at all the possible ways a logo can interact with a specific space, screen, and person. While designing Mary Jo Hansons logo, I developed hundreds of variations, to make sure the logo was properly adjusted for every medium it would be displayed on and intereacted well with useres.

Logo & Business Card

The final logo and business card is designed to follow a strict grid so that the structure of the copy can easily be peeled back when scaling the logo to smaller widths. The aesthetic of both designs portrays Mary Jo as a reliable and knowledgeable advocate for the people she defends.
